JAWS

"Jaws" is a classic thriller film directed by Steven Spielberg. The story is set in the fictional Amity Island, where a great white shark terrorizes the community during the summer tourist season. As the attacks escalate, Police Chief Martin Brody, marine biologist Matt Hooper, and seasoned shark hunter Quint embark on a perilous journey to capture and kill the deadly predator.

PEARL

“Pearl” is a 2022 American horror film directed by Ti West, co-written by West and Mia Goth, who reprises her role as the title character. A prequel to X (2022) and the second installment in the X film series, it serves as an origin story for the title villain, whose fervent aspiration to become a movie star led her to committing violent acts on her family's Texas homestead in 1918.

RAIDERS OF THE LOST ARK

"Raiders of the Lost Ark" (1981) is an action-adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg and produced by George Lucas. Set in 1936, the story follows the iconic archaeologist Indiana Jones, played by Harrison Ford, as he embarks on a quest to find the mystical Ark of the Covenant before the Nazis do. SUSPIRIA

"Suspiria" is a psychological horror film directed by Luca Guadagnino. The story is set in Berlin in 1977 and follows Susie Bannion, an ambitious young dancer from Ohio who joins the prestigious Markos Tanz Company. Unbeknownst to Susie, the dance academy is a front for a coven of witches. "Suspiria" is a reimagining of Dario Argento's 1977 film of the same name.
