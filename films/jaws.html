JAWS

"Jaws" is a classic thriller film directed by Steven Spielberg. The story is set in the fictional Amity Island, where a great white shark terrorizes the community during the summer tourist season. As the attacks escalate, Police Chief Martin Brody, marine biologist Matt Hooper, and seasoned shark hunter Quint embark on a perilous journey to capture and kill the deadly predator. The film explores the tension between the characters and the relentless pursuit of the monstrous shark, building suspense and fear throughout. "Jaws" is known for its iconic score, directed by John Williams, and its impact on the thriller genre.

Horror, Thriller 2 h 4 m 1975 USA
